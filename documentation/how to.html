How to:

How does Sign up logic work?

Prompt Eingineer SaaS registration process is designed to offer both trial perioid or directly force your client into paid one. Main controll for trial perioid can overide your entire plans you have created if you navigate to Admin=>Settings=> General settings. Its registration logic allows user to create their account, cnfigure plan and eventualy buy it. Sign up logic is predifned to let customer in app and than purchase plan, while it keeps prompt plans and its content locked along with AI tools if you set it up like this. Also App have abbility to set up free prompts tab/category wich does not consume tokens but thats entierly up to you. It can also enable trial perioid but keep in mind regardless how you set plan it will make prompts across all plans open and availible to everyone during designated perioid.

How do tokens work?

Token feauture is designed to give admins more controll over prompt value, amount how much it can export or use in system and also its goal and purpose is to increase revenue

Every prompt client decides to discover consumes 1 token out of his balance. Tokens will also recharge after month expires but only in case your client pays for plan as this is subscription based app

How do trial tokens work?

Trial tokens work the same way as paid plan ones, but they have limit you can set in billing plans and after they are used client needs to purchase plan or tokens to discover and use more prompts in app

How does prompt use/discover function works?

User can discover prompt by clicking over it on discover button. Once your client clicks on discover it will consume 1 token and store this prompt in collections tab, from where client can click on it and use it in AI tools or copy it. More about copy/export feauture and limitation you can find later in this documentation.

How can i allow/disalow export of prompts in app.

Simply put, you can controll what plan can copy AI prompt content in billing>plans. Turning on copy button will allow user within specific plan to copy AI prompt, while when you turn it off it will block copy to clipboard functionality. For now we designed system like this untill we collect more feedback what would our clients like to do.
